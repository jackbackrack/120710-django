About This Gallery Management System

Open-source Django gallery management application. Manages artists, artworks, exhibitions (shows), events, and juror reviews/ratings with public-facing pages, a role-based admin workflow, Mailchimp mailing list integration, and Schema.org JSON-LD structured data on every content page.

Features

Artists

Profile pages with bio, statement, image, website, Instagram, and linked artworks

Artworks

Detail pages with medium, dimensions, pricing, Schema.org `VisualArtwork` structured data

Shows (Exhibitions)

M2M artists and curators, artworks inline, open call support with artist submission workflow, Schema.org `VisualArtsEvent`

Open Call Workflow

Artists submit artworks to open call shows before the submission deadline. After the deadline the show enters jury review phase. Curators select or reject submissions, then promote selected artworks into the show — triggering acceptance/rejection emails to submitting artists.

Events

Linked to shows with date/time, Schema.org `VisualArtsEvent` with `superEvent`

Reviews and ratings

Per-show juror assignments with one 1 to 10 rating and optional review notes per juror per artwork. Curators see average scores and review counts on the submissions page.

Role-based access

Log in and inherit various roles as asigned by gallery staff - `artist`, `curator`, `juror`, `staff` groups with granular permissions and role-specific documentation on this page. When staff promote a linked artist account to curator, that artist profile is set to public.

Sign In

Local sign in (username/password) or Google OAuth via django-allauth

Admin

Staff role(s) with artwork inline and filter_horizontal for artists/curators; CSV/Excel import-export on all models
