How To Set Up Your Artist User Account -- by invitation only now

  1. in browser go to 120710
  2. click on Forgot Your Password
  3. type in your email address (that you gave curator)
  4. check email (and optionally spam folder) and find message from us and reset password link
  5. click on reset link in email message
  6. change password by typing new password twice and hitting change password button
  7. click on Sign In with your email address and new password
  8. that will take you to the home page

Add/Update Artist Information

if you have already shown at 120710 and _HAVE_ an artist card then

  1. click on Artists
  2. find yourself in the view by using find in your browser
  3. click on your name in your artist card to go to your artist info
  4. click edit at bottom of your card if you don't like it

else you are new to 120710...

  1. click on New to make a new artist

from there...

  1. fill in or update your more information (the more information you provide the more users will see of you and your work)
  2. when done, hit save
  3. then you'll see your artist info
  4. make sure it is to your liking
  5. you can hit the edit link (at the bottom of your artist card) to update it

Add Artwork Information

  1. click on new link below Artworks at bottom of your Artist page
  2. add information for your artwork
  3. hit save when done editing
  4. see how it looks and edit until done
  5. you can edit it by clicking on edit link at the bottom of your artwork card
  6. then click on your name in the artist info to get back to your artist view
  7. you will see all your artworks below
  8. add more artworks by clicking on new until done
